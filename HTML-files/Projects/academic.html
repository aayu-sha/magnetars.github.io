Simulation Results

This section includes graphs and images simulated for the paper, illustrating the impact of dark energy and dark matter interactions on galaxy formation.

  • Figure 1 shows the expansion of the universe with w=1, highlighting dark energy's role.
  • Figure 2 displays the CMB power spectrum, reflecting the influence of dark matter.
  • Figure 3 presents the Hubble diagram of supernovae, used to measure cosmic expansion.
  • Figure 4 visualizes a simulation of galaxy formation under dark matter-dark energy interactions.
  • Figure 5 compares simulation results with the standard cosmological model.
  • Figure 6 illustrates the evolution of galaxy mass with and without dark energy-dark matter interactions.
