Celestial Mechanics Simulations

Overview

These projects, carried out under the mentorship of Professor Seppo Mikkola, introduced us to advanced celestial mechanics simulations. By studying and experimenting with his codes, we built a strong foundation in N-body dynamics and the numerical techniques used in astrophysical research.

Lyapunov Exponent Analysis

Investigated the stability and chaotic behavior of dynamical systems using Lyapunov exponents. Implemented algorithms to compute and visualize results in Python, gaining hands-on experience in analyzing system stability and chaos theory.
