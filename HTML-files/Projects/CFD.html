CFD Internship at IISc Bangalore

Overview

During our internship at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore (Dec 20, 2024 - Feb 7, 2025), under Prof. S.V. Raghurama Rao, Dr. Aravind C.B., and Dr. Kedar Wagh, we explored computational fluid dynamics (CFD) by implementing numerical solvers (LLF, Roe, Godunov) for hyperbolic PDEs. We simulated test cases like linear advection, Burgers' equation, and dam break problems, visualizing results with ParaView. This collaborative project by Aayusha Singh (numerical implementation, visualization) and Amrit Roy (theoretical analysis, code optimization) advanced our expertise in aerospace engineering.
